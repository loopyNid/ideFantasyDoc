IDE_Fantasy

IDE Fantasy: Telematic Dance for 3 Performers with IMU sensors, SuperCollider and Godot Gaming Engine

The whole IDE Fantasy performance is combines Supercollider for audio production, the Godot game engine for graphics and Hamachi vpn + osc to achieve the telematic nature of the piece.

This is the technical implementation of the the sensor glove the dancers wear in the performance.

The glove uses the Raspberry pi zero w because of its open source and connectivity benefits. Utilizing the the Raspap project for ease of use.
